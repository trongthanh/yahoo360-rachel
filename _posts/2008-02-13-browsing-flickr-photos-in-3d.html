--- author: ttt_conan title: 'Browsing flickr photos in 3D' date: 2008-02-13T08:05:57+07:00 --- In a few days free from client projects, I have done some studies and made this demo. With this embeddable flash application, you can browse your and your friends' flickr public photos with a stunning 3D experience. (up to 50 latest photos)

Flickr + PaperCloud = FlickrCloud





Technical info:
This application is the combination of
- Flash Player 9 (Action Script 3)
- Flickr API (with the help of Adobe AS3 Flickr Library)
- Papervision3D engine.
- Original 3D effect from PaperCloud demo (included in Papervision3D classes and examples).
