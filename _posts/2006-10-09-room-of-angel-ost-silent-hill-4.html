This song is an OST from the game Silent Hill 4 - The Room (a famous thriller/horror game). It's melody sounds mysterious and a little bit scary (and of course it fits the settings of the game). The lyrics are scary as well. It's hard to describe my feeling when I listen to this song, something uncertain, something profound.
